The Walk

The walk from Monterrosso to Riomaggiore will take you approximately tow hours, give or take an hour depending on the weather conditions and your physical shape.

What?

Cinque Terre comprises five villages: Monterosso, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore

Where?

On the northwest cost of the Italian Riviera, north of the city La Spezia

Price?

The Walk is free!
